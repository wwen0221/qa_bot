Company Name:

Zephyra Dynamics

Company Description:
Zephyra Dynamics is a next-generation tech and sustainability company founded in 2014 and headquartered in Helsinki, Finland. They specialize in AI-powered environmental solutions, modular robotics, and smart energy storage systems for urban and industrial applications. Their mission is to accelerate the transition toward self-sustaining smart cities by combining cutting-edge data science, energy efficiency, and autonomous systems.

Core Values:
	•	Innovation Without Boundaries
	•	Sustainability First
	•	Transparent Impact

Notable Achievements:
	•	Winner of the 2023 Nordic Sustainability Innovation Award
	•	Partnered with the European Space Agency for autonomous climate monitoring satellites
	•	Carbon-neutral certified since 2021
